Before You Email

  • For purchase issues, try Restore Purchases in the app first.
  • For local model issues, confirm the model finished downloading and is selected in Models.
  • For progress recovery, use the Profile recovery export/import tools if available.
  • Include your device model, OS version, and app version when reporting a bug.
